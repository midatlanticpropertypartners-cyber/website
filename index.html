Our Green Philosophy

Our Eco-Promise: We Donate & Recycle First

We believe junk doesn't belong in Maryland's landfills if it can be salvaged. We sort through every truckload, pulling out furniture, housewares, electronics, and construction material for recycling or local charity donation.

Community Donations

Good quality furniture, clothing, and working electronics are delivered straight to local shelters and thrift organizations in Anne Arundel County.

Rigorous Recycling

Metals, cardboard, plastics, and appliances are separated and sent to certified local recycling processors.

Responsible Disposal

Only actual, non-recyclable garbage is safely disposed of at licensed transfer stations in compliance with local eco-regulations.
